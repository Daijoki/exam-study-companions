Limited IRB Review and Broad Consent

Limited IRB Review applies to four exemption categories, in which the IRB must determine whether the privacy protections and data confidentiality are adequate or whether the broad consent requirements are met. Expedited review procedures should be used to conduct limited IRB review.

Exemption Categories 7 and 8 are exempt from broad consent because they relate to the storage of identifiable private information or identifiable biospecimens for secondary research and for secondary research use of such identifiable information.
