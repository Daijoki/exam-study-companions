IRB Composition Requirements

Requirement 45 CFR 46.107 21 CFR 56.107
Minimum members At least five members At least five members
Varying backgrounds Members with varying backgrounds to promote complete and adequate review of research activities commonly conducted by the institution Members of varying backgrounds to promote complete and adequate review of research activities
Professional competence Members with professional competence necessary to review specific research activities Sufficient qualifications and experience, including consideration of race, gender, cultural backgrounds, and sensitivity to such issues as community attitudes
Gender diversity Not entirely of one gender Not entirely men or women
Non-scientist member At least one member whose primary concerns are in nonscientific areas At least one member whose primary concerns are in nonscientific areas
Community member At least one member who is not otherwise affiliated with the institution and who is not part of the immediate family of a person who is affiliated with the institution At least one member who is not otherwise affiliated with the institution and who is not part of the immediate family of a person who is affiliated with the institution

Conflicts of interest No member may participate in the IRB's initial or continuing review of any project in which the member has a conflicting interest, except to provide information requested by the IRB No member shall vote on any project in which the member has a conflicting interest, except to provide information requested by the IRB
Quorum In order for the research to be approved, it shall receive the approval of a majority of those members present at a convened meeting at which a majority of the members of the IRB are present In order for the research to be approved, it shall receive the approval of a majority of those members present at the meeting
